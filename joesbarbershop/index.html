Joe's

Barbershop

Your neighborhood barbershop for over 60 years!

After the war, Indiana-native Joseph Clemens migrated west in search of modest prosperity and adventure. Having moonlighted as a navy barber, Joe answered his calling (and avoided the shafts), and opened up the first reputable barber shop Leadville had seen in over a decade.

Since then, Joe's Barbershop (still family owned) has served miners and their descendants for over 60 years. Enjoy the best family-friendly service this side of the Rockies. No appointment necessary.
